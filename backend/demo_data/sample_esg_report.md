# Sample ESG Report Excerpt

NVIDIA's FY2025 sustainability update states that the company remains committed to reducing greenhouse gas emissions across operations and its value chain.

In 2024, NVIDIA reported a 14% reduction in scope 2 market-based emissions. The company also expanded renewable energy procurement and set a target to reach 100% renewable electricity for selected sites.

The board's governance policy requires quarterly oversight of climate risk and AI safety topics. Management identified transition risk associated with energy demand growth in data centers.

NVIDIA announced a new water management initiative to reduce freshwater use in office operations and support long-term resilience goals.
